About the Rankings

Why GDP and Market Cap? We rank fiat by GDP and Bitcoin, Gold, and Silver by market cap for a fair comparison. The table shows Bitcoin, Gold, Silver, and the top 30 currencies by GDP. GDP data is sourced from the IMF's World Economic Outlook database.

Data Sources

  • Bitcoin: Real-time price and market cap from CoinGecko API
  • Gold & Silver: Prices derived from Bitcoin-to-Gold and Bitcoin-to-Silver ratios via CoinGecko API, with market caps calculated using:
    • Gold: 215,000 metric tons of estimated above-ground supply
    • Silver: 1,800,000 metric tons of estimated above-ground supply
  • GDP Data: IMF World Economic Outlook database (via proxy API)
  • Eurozone: Combined GDP of all 20 Euro-using countries:
